KPMG Virtual Internship Projects

Scenario: The client Sprocket Central Pty Ltd specializes in high-quality bikes and accessible cycling accessories for riders. Their marketing team is looking to boost business by analyzing customer demographic. The 3 Phases of Analysis (Data Exploration, Model Development, and Interpretation) will then be utilized.

Module 1: Data Quality Assessment in MS Word and Excel

The first task includes data cleaning and drafting an email regarding data quality issues and strategies. The datasets provided for this task are: customer demographic, customer address, and transactions (past 3 months). The 6 data quality dimensions are also utilized to improve the data.
